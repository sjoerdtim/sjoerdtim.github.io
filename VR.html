Virtual Reality

Virtual Reality brings us the next step for our digital world. I believe that with VR, we can improve the quality of life for all.

Below you will find my experience working with VR.







My Experience



CREATING APPLICATIONS


I can create various VR applications from the ground up using Unity3D. I am capable of doing this using both SteamVR and OculusVR, or a wrapper for both.
CONVERTING APPLICATIONS


I can convert applications from the SteamVR to the OculusVR SDK for multiple devices and the other way around and have done so on many occasions.
DEMOS AND SUPPORT


I am capable of giving VR demos to people familiar and unfamiliar with VR and to guide them to using it correctly, while exciting them to the possiblities.
