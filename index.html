Hello! I'm a developer from the Netherlands specialized in game development, virtual reality, and augmented reality using C++ and C#.

Below you will find my work, skills, and resume.









What I Do




GAME DEVELOPMENT


Game Development is something that has long been my passion. During the last 4 years, I have worked on games for both mobile and PC games using C++ and C#. My experience also includes leading a programming team.



More on Games
VIRTUAL REALITY


One of my biggest interests is Virtual Reality. I have spent a year working with some of the most innovative minds working on VR and during that time I gained a lot of experience developing for both Oculus and SteamVR.



More on VR
AUGMENTED REALITY


Much like Virtual Reality, I believe there is a big future in Augmented Reality. Not just for games, but also for use in our daily lives to make things easier. I have worked on many AR projects and this has made me familiar with using ARCore and ARKit.

About Me

Ever since I was a child, I've been intrigued with making video games. After years of practice, I joined the Breda University of Applied Sciences to study Game Programming.

In my final year there, I had an internship at KLM Royal Dutch Airlines, where I was introduced to Virtual and Augmented Reality. Working with this has given me a new passion, to introduce the world to the possibilities of VR and AR, through games, or through other means.

My goal for the future: Help changing the world using this new technology.
