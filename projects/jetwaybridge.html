ABOUT THE APPLICATION
The Jetway Bridge training is a training that was developed by KLM Digital Studios to train bridge connection. The initial training was made to work with SteamVR, which means they needed a pc to run the training.

KLM wanted to extend this application to support the Oculus Quest, which meant it had to be converted from SteamVR to OculusVR. This was one part of my tasks. The second task was to create a disconnect training.

MY RESPONSIBILITIES
The original project was created by Digtal Studios. My work consisted of two seperate tasks: converting the project from SteamVR to OculusVR, and adding a bridge disconnect training mode. The initial bridge connect training was not made by me.

My responsibilities during this project:
  • Converting the project from SteamVR to OculusVR
  • Adding a menu screen where the trainer can select a training type
  • Adding functionality for autopark button
  • Adding a button with which the trainer can make a car drive through the red zone
